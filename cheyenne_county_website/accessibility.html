Accessibility

  • At Cheyenne County, we are dedicated to ensuring equitable access to all our services for all Coloradans. We are committed to meeting the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, AAA level criteria, which ensures our technology is accessible to users with sensory, cognitive, and mobility disabilities, benefiting all users, regardless of ability.

  • Our Commitment

  • Continuously strive to improve the accessibility of our site.
  • Make our content accessible to everyone, regardless of disability.
  • Welcome feedback to help us further enhance accessibility and accommodate diverse needs.

  • How you can help

  • Share your suggestions or accommodation requests by emailing us at ccadmin@rebeltec.net.

Guidelines

  • The Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA)
  • Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1
  • Your feedback is instrumental in our ongoing efforts to create a more inclusive site.

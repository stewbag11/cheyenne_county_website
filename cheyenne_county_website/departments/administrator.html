  • Responsibilities

  • The County Administrator works closely with all County departments to implement policies as directed by the Board of County Commissioners and to monitor the cost-effectiveness of operations. The Administrator also coordinates meetings of the Board of County Commissioners. As the chief administrative officer of the County, the Administrator is responsible for the effective administration of all governmental affairs of the County that legally may be placed in the Administrator's charge. The Administrator shall implement and enforce ordinances, policies, rules, and regulations adopted by the Board of County Commissioners.

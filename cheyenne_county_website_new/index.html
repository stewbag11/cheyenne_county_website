Welcome

We thank you for visiting Cheyenne County, Colorado! We will be updating our site frequently with our meeting agendas, county activities, and job openings. You are invited to browse our site to learn more about our county history, officials, departments and more.


The town of Cheyenne Wells is the County seat and home of the Cheyenne County Courthouse. The towns of Kit Carson and Cheyenne Wells are incorporated, and the smaller towns of Arapahoe and Wild Horse remain unincorporated.

Firstview Landfill

  • 151 S 1st St W, Cheyenne Wells, CO 80810
  • Phone: (719)-340-5099

  • Manager / Operator

  • Mark Galli

  • Hours

  • Tuesday, Wednesday, Friday
  • 8:00 am - 1:00 pm (MT)
  • Saturday
  • 8:00 am - 4:00 pm (MT)
  • Closed
  • Legal holidays & High wind days

  • Policies

  • • No out-of-county waste permitted.
  • • Loads must be covered with a tarp, bagged, or secured.
  • • Unsecured loads will be assessed double fees.
  • • Construction materials must be sorted into categories: wood, steel, concrete, asphalt, cardboard, sheet rock, insulation, etc.
  • • Payment for non-commercial loads is required at the gate before dumping.
  • • Commercial vendors can establish a 30-day charge account.
  • • Dead animals and auto bodies are prohibited.
  • • Hay net, hay/bale twine, volleyball nets, and any other type of netting, as well as plastic tarps, must be separated and compacted.
  • • Electronic Waste (E-Waste) must be sorted and placed into E-Waste bins.

  • Rates

  • Vehicles

  • • Autos, vans, & SUVs: $5.00
  • • Pickups & one tons: $7.50
  • • Pickup box trailer or similar size: $7.50
  • • Single axle trucks & all trailers mounted with light truck tires: $15.00
  • • Tandem axle truck: $35.00
  • • Semi-load: $75.00

  • Tires

  • • Car tires: $3.00 each
  • • Light truck tires: $4.00 each
  • • Truck tires: $10.00 each
  • • Tractor tires: $35.00 each

  • Other Items

  • • Batteries: $2.00 each
  • • TV's, monitors, and microwaves: $5.00 each
  • • Roll-offs: $50.00 each
  • • Commercial haulers/compacted loads: $4.00 per cubic yard
  • • Appliances (refrigerators, freezers, & air conditioners without a Freon Free Certification): $10.00 each
  • • Mattresses, toilets, sinks, & furniture: $5.00 each
  • • Approved contaminated soils: $150/load
  • • Trailer houses (with a phase 1 asbestos abatement certificate): $500 each
  • • Other Large Items: prices to be negotiated

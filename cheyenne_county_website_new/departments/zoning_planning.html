Registration & Safety Requirements

The Planning and Zoning Board and Board of Commissioners strongly suggest wind companies register wind farm latitude/longitude sites on both the county website and the Colorado Agriculture Aviation Association (CAAA) website. Aviation buoys are required on the upper 1/3 portion of the towers for conditional use applications to be approved. Reflection tape is suggested.
